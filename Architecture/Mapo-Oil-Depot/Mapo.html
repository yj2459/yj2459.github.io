Rehabilitating Mapo Oil Depot



Ranging from the most radical to the most conservative, five different methods of dealing with these ruins will be practiced within the five tanks. Each tank will become a paradigm of a certain approach, where all the approaches are fundamentally about regenerating Industrial ruin. These tanks will become precedents of regenerative architecture, educating the public of different solutions on preserving the ruins.
